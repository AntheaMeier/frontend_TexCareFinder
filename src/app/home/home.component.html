Do you get skin irritations from textile lables? Or are you annoyed that most textile care instructions are printed rather than embroidered and lose their print after a few washes?

Stop having skin irritations from lables or to worry about forgetting the recommended care after a while. TexCare Finder helps to to keep your textiles in top condition by allowing you to store and manage all of your care instructions in one place.

How to use this App?
Create a new textile item to build up your database of care instructions.
View all of your items and edit them when needed.

Enjoy having access to your care instructions
at any time and from anywhere.
